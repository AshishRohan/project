The Budget Mirror

Welcome to the Government Budget Transparency Portal, your comprehensive source for accessing and understanding the financial operations of the government. Our mission is to foster openness, accountability, and public engagement by providing transparent and easy-to-understand information about how public funds are allocated, spent, and managed.

This portal serves as a centralized hub for all budget-related information, including the annual Union and State Budgets, detailed breakdowns of sectoral spending, real-time updates on budget implementations, and reports on fiscal performance. We aim to empower citizens, policymakers, researchers, and civil society by making budgetary data accessible, promoting informed public discourse on national and regional financial priorities.

Through this platform, you can explore:

Annual Budget Documents: Comprehensive reports on government expenditures and revenue projections.

Expenditure Tracking: Real-time updates on where and how public funds are being utilized

Audit Reports: Insights from the Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG) on budget performance and accountability.

Analytical Tools: Visual dashboards and data analytics to help you dive deeper into fiscal trends and sectoral allocations.

Our commitment is to enhance financial transparency, ensure accountability, and encourage participatory governance by keeping you informed about how your tax money is used for the country’s development.
